About Us

Welcome to Flipkart Clone – your one-stop destination for the best online shopping experience!

We are a passionate team of developers and designers recreating the look, feel, and core features of Flipkart to provide a realistic e-commerce project experience for learning and demonstration purposes.

Whether you're browsing for electronics, fashion, or daily essentials, our platform is designed to showcase:

  • Modern frontend design with Bootstrap
  • Functional shopping cart and booking systems
  • Product filtering, searching, and user profiles
  • Admin control panel and more

This project is built using Python Django on the backend and is ideal for developers learning full-stack development, building portfolios, or teaching real-world concepts.

Contact us: If you have any questions, suggestions, or want to contribute, feel free to reach out on our Contact Page.
